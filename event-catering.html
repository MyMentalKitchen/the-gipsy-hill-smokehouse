Corporate Events

Event catering services for every occasion — meetings, conferences, staff parties and product launches.

Good Craic

The reason for having an event is to provide a quality guest experience. It requires great food, a good atmosphere and smooth logistics.

We specialize in making that experience a reality. For some guests at a hog roast, good crackling from a slow roast cooked to perfection is all it takes for them to rave about the event.

Using a porchetta rosemary, fennel and garlic recipe with extra virgin olive oil, we marinate the meat while the spit roast cooks.

Experienced Service

Our experience encompasses service at all types of events — from business meetings and conferences to weddings and birthdays. We are experts in our field, specialising in barbecuing and spit roasts.

Tim Clements, the business owner, was brought up in Jamaica and Portugal, where barbecuing and Al Fresco living is just the way of life. We import and distribute fine Portuguese black pig charcuterie, smoked products and preserves.

We began roasting whole free-range hogs marinated with our unique mix of herbs and spices. Our professional catering for events started in 2004.

Fantastic Food

Our packages range from basic to luxurious, so whether you're looking for simple finger food or a five-course meal, we have the perfect catering package.

Our team of expert caterers can make it happen no matter what kind of cuisine or dietary requirements you have.

We cater smoked beef brisket and spicy pork ribs over ash and Irish peat. Other foods include pulled pork, chicken, burgers, and different American and Portuguese style BBQ foods — meats and sides.

Quality

We roast free-range hogs marinated with our unique mix of herbs and spices, sourced from Blythburgh Farm in Suffolk.

All our food comes from local suppliers and is prepared from our establishment which has a food hygiene rating of 5 — Scores on the Doors.
