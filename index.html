Roast Hog has been a trailblazer in the hog roasting catering industry since 2004, providing mouthwatering spit-roasted hogs to hundreds of events. Originally the hog roast caterer at Borough Market in London Bridge, we've catered at street markets, music festivals, charity events including Gordon Ramsay's F-Word, and at The Fat Duck in Bray. Based in South East London, we focus on the London area and travel nationwide for special occasions.
