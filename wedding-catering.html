Welcome to The Gipsy Hill Smokehouse

We are a hog roast and wedding catering company based in Gipsy Hill, London. Our reputation as a hog roast caterer has grown as we built the business over almost 20 years.

The Gipsy Hill Smokehouse is a family-run business. Our expert chefs specialize in creating mouth-watering hog roasts for all occasions, from wedding receptions and corporate events to intimate family gatherings.

We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service, professionalism and versatility. Our hog roast is widely considered to be the best-tasting around.

We are proud to be an approved supplier for Club Langley banqueting services, Oaks Farm wedding events and Beaverwood event planners.

Our Hog Roasts, Spit Roasts & Barbecues

Our ethos for sourcing our food is to only use the highest-quality ingredients in all of our hog roasts, spit roasts, barbecues and side dishes.

We supply canapes and offer bar services to provide reception welcome drinks. Our main course of spit roast and barbecue meats include pork served with delicious crackling, lamb, beef, and chicken.

We ensure that vegetarians and those with dietary requirements are especially served. The desserts we offer come from artisan bakers and will delight your guests.

Our Origin and Story Since 2004

Renowned for the quality and distinctive taste of our free range hog roasts marinated porchetta-style, we have earned quite a few credits we are proud to call our achievements.

We traded as Roast Hog at Borough Market in London Bridge from 2005, cooking for anniversaries and community days.

We've catered at Kensington Palace, for Tomasina Miers' (Young Masterchef 2006) book launch with Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall, and on Gordon Ramsay's F-Word. We were honoured to cook for Heston Blumenthal's team at The Fat Duck, Bray.

The Geographical Area We Cover

We cover a 50-mile radius around the London area, from North London to South East London. Established as a London hog roast company, we focus our hog roasting in the local areas — but please do enquire as we have travelled as far as Scone in Scotland, Cornwall, and even to Portugal and the South of Spain.
